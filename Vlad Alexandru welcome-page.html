Founded in 1852 the American wagon and automobile manufacturer based in South Bend, Indiana, with a building at 1600 Broadway, Times Square, Midtown Manhattan, New York City. Beggining with manufacturing wagons, buggies, carriages and harnesses the company had a safe slow rising as times changed and Studebaker managed to build light cars throughout the representative car construction periods, the 50's and the 60's.

More than one hundred years of innovation spread around America and such lovely cruisers must be seen in open-air exhibitions and you can take a ride!
